Our Industry Solutions

Delivering the Best Global Industry Services and Products.

HFC Oil & Gas Ltd. (“HFC” or “Company”) is an international oil and gas company engaged in the exploration and development of unconventional oil and gas assets. The Company’s principal interests are located in three major underexplored base in USA, UK and Canada; with further interests in Hungary, covering approximately 12.3 million gross acres. The Company’s interests are in countries with a high regional demand for energy and for USA, Canada and UK are close to existing infrastructure allowing for rapid delivery of oil and gas to market.
